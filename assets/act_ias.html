About IAS academy

VIETW is to increase awareness of competition exam on an individual human scale, and offer equally simple solutions for success in those exams, which offer unique coaching for TNPSC Group 1, TNPSC Group 2, TNPSC Group 4, TNPSC VAO Exam, DEO Exam, AE Exam, POLICE, CA-CPT, IAS, IBPS(Bank Exam), RRB, SSC, SPOKEN ENGLISH, SCHOOL and COLLEGE ACCOUNTS.

we offer intensive and quality coaching Through VETRII IAS STUDY CIRCLE to students appearing for civil service examination conducted by the Union Public Service Commission and the Group I and II examination conducted by TNPSC at the Preliminary, Mains and Interview level.VETRII IAS academy coaching centre is a committed team of faculty who have been involved in Civil Service preparation for the past “10 YEARS” and have been instrumental for the success of many candidates in their Civil Service exam Preparation.

Our coaching center aims to change the life of students through conscious training with individual care all at time. We are continuously growing, since we maintain small group per batch with lowest fee structure.

Vision & Mission

Vivekanandha Educational Institutions aspires to be recognised as one of the leaders in engineering education, research and application of knowledge to benefit society. Provide world-class Engineering Education, Foster Research and Development. Evolve innovative applications of Technology. Encourage Entrepreneurship. Ultimately mould young women capable of assuming leadership of the society for the betterment of the Country. Our institution prides itself as an institution that is quick to adapt its learning delivery platform to rapidly evolving learning environments. This has resulted in creating a vibrant ambience on campus that engages students in a variety of ways.

We at Vivekanandha institute of engineering and technology for women strive to achieve customer satisfaction by providing Quality Education and Training Science in Engineering, Technology and Management in congenial and disciplined environment through various Union Public Service Commission training classes. Around 84 students were involved in this training class.
