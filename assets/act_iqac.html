Internal Quality Assurance Cell - Quality Policy

We, at Vivekanandha Institute of Engineering and Technology for Women are committed to deliver Quality Technical Education in a conducive teaching learning environment by adapting appropriate processes, tools and techniques for the holistic development of our Students.

Quality Objectives

The Primary aim of IQAC is

  • To develop a system for conscious, consistent and catalytic action to improve the academic and administrative performance of the Institution
  • To promote measures for institutional functioning towards quality enhancement through internalization of quality culture and institutionalization of best practices

Internal Quality Assurance Cell - Tasks @ IQAC

  • Women Empowerment
  • Promotion of Human Values and Professional Ethics
  • Fostering Excellence in Research
  • Energy and Environment Management
  • Student Support and Progression
  • Effective Teaching-Learning and Evaluation
  • ICT for Learning and Support and administrative services
