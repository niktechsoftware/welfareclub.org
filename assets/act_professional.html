Oracle

Oracle In the year of 2009 an MOU signed between oracle university-Delhi and Vivekanandha Institute of Engineering and Technology for Women-Tiruchengode.

This agreement facilitates our institution to conduct workforce development program (WDP) which enables the student to get placed in top most It companies.

The Oracle Workforce Development Program is directed and headed by department of CSE.

Mr.K.Sankar,AP/CSE is the coordinator of the oracle training program, who had taken training in oracle database log at oracle university-Delhi in the year of 2009.

From the year of 2009 the oracle workforce development program is in effective and trained each and every batch of the students.

Oracle workforce development program consisting of two modules:

Module 1:

Oracle database log: Introduction to SQL 1

Module 2:

Oracle database log: Administration workshop 1
