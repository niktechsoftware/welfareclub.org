Anti Ragging

 

Anti Ragging

Forms of Ragging

  • Dress Code Ragging
  • Formal Introduction
  • Verbal Torture
  • Playing the Fool
  • Sexual Abuse
  • Hostel Ragging
  • Drug Abuse

Punishments

  • Cancellation of admission
  • Suspension from attending classes
  • Withdrawal of scholarship and other benefits
  • Not allowed to attend any examination
  • Debarring from representing the Institution in any regional, national or international meet
  • Suspension/Expulsion from the hostel
  • Rustication from the Institution
  • Collective punishment as a deterrent to ensure community pressure on the potential raggers
  • FIR with the police and arrest
