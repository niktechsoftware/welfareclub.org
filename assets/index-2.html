News & Events

Welcome to
Vivekanandha Institute of Engineering & Technology for Women

VIETW was started in the year 2006 to educate, promote and shape girl students into capable engineers. The aim of establishing this college is to provide quality education by using cutting edge technology and make them as technocrats, scientists and entrepreneurs of the modern world.

Importance is given to the job oriented practical knowledge to make students to face the challenges in the competitive world.
