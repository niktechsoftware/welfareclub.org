Master of Computer Applications

About the Department

The Department of Computer Applications was established in the year 2007 offers PG programme Master of Computer Applications with an intake of 60 students.

Computer has become an indispensable part of any business organization or an educational Institution. You name any concept and it is in no time analyzed and transferred in to a memory chip. In order to meet the growing need for trained computer professionals we periodically update computer lab with the latest software and hardware matching the best in the Industry. The department mainly focuses on the industrial application of the subject taught. It will not be an over-statement to say that it is nice to be part of this growing and glowing culture.

The department conducts the value added courses conducted by leading software giants like IBM, ORACLE etc., to enhance the knowledge of the students and get placed in the leading MNC’s.

The department has achieved 100% results in all the 7 outgone batches from 2007 to 2013.

Vision

To be an academic institution exclusively for women, in dynamic equilibrium with the social and economic environment, striving continuously for excellence in education, research and technological service to the society.

Mission

To discover, teach and apply knowledge for the intellectual, cultural, ethical, social and economic growth of women students.

Facilities

The department is well equipped with latest servers with 120 client systems, 18.5” TFT monitors and Multimedia. The lab has a rich collection of Softwares covering wide range of applications. The students are provided laptops with WIFI connectivity, so that the students are connected to the Internet 24 x 7. The department has all the facilities to conduct the following labs

  • DBMS Laboratory
  • Data Structures and Algorithms Laboratory
  • Communication Skill Laboratory
  • Object Oriented Programming Laboratory
  • Web Programming Laboratory
  • Graphics and Multimedia Laboratory
  • Embedded Systems Laboratory
  • Internet Programming Laboratory
  • Visual Programming Laboratory
  • Software Development- Case Tools Laboratory
  • Network Programming Laboratory
  • Technical Seminar and Report Writing
  • Advanced Internet Programming Laboratory
  • XML and Web Services Laboratory
