Students Achievement

Co-Curricular Activities

Equal importance is given to academic activities and personal development programmes of each student in VIETW. Students are given vital roles in Event Management activities and preparation of study materials on different topics with the help of internet, reference books, journals and e-learning methods.

Besides, regular quiz programmes, news and views, extempore speech, public speaking, debates, role plays, group discussions, case-analysis, economic trend analysis brain storming sessions, presentations, MDPs, management games, book reviews, psychometric tests etc., are some of the routine events organized in VIETW. The personal development of students are monitored ,evaluated and corrected by faculty members on a regular basis.
