B.E. Electrical and Electronics Engineering

Facilities

Laboratory Details

The department has the following five well equipped laboratories and a workshop with state-of-the-art facilities:

  • DC Machines Laboratory
  • AC Machines Laboratory
  • Power Electronics Laboratory
  • Power System Simulation Laboratory
  • Electron devices and circuits Laboratory
  • Measurements & Instrumentation Lab
  • Microprocessors and Microcontroller Laboratory
  • Control System Laboratory
  • Linear Integrated Circuit Laboratory
  • Control and Instrumentation Laboratory
  • Engineering Practices Laboratory
  • Electronics Laboratory
  • Research Laboratory
Machines Laboratory

This laboratory imparts the knowledge about characteristics and behavior of the DC and AC Machines. Well established with all kinds of motors, generators and latest Drives. This is the core lab for the Electrical Engineering branch and the ancillary for ECE, CSE and IT.


Specifications:

  • DC Generators
  • DC Motor Generator set
  • Induction Motor
  • DC Shunt, series and compound motor sets
  • Single phase and three phase transformers
  • Auto Transformer - Single Phase and Three Phase
  • Measuring Instruments, Loading Instruments
  • AC Motors - Single Phase and Three Phases
  • Transformer - Single Phase and Three Phases
  • Auto Transformer - Single Phase and Three Phases
  • Measuring Instruments, Loading Instruments
  • SPWM Inverters
  • Induction generator
  • Linear Induction Motor
  • Synchronous Motors
  • Alternators
Power Electronics & Microprocessor Laboratory

It consists of different kinds of demo kits such as SCR characteristics, study kit for D.C motor control using chopper and necessary simulation software's. The lab is equipped with all the power electronic converters, DSP and FPGA based DC and AC Drives, Digital Integrated kits, Power supplies etc.


Specifications:

  • Microprocessor Kits
  • Microcontroller Kits
  • Brushless Motor - AVS Drive
  • SCR Circuit breakers
  • Bridge and Semiconductor firing circuits
  • PWM Converter
  • IC tester
  • EPROM Programmer and PLC
  • Power Converters and Power Quality Analyzer
  • Digital Storage Oscilloscope
Control System Laboratory

This Lab introduces about the fundamentals of control systems theory with emphasis on design and implementation to the students. The lab focuses on technical implementation issues of classical control theory in the frequency domain and modern control theory in the state-space. Design and implementation of control theory is done using MATLAB software with control system toolbox to undergo the experiments like open loop and closed loop system. The lab was well furnished with the equipments like AC and DC Servomotor, Synchros, position control system, Controller design kit, LVDT,Thermistors, Thermocouple and RTD kit. The lab provides the facility to analyze the stability of the system. The lab is also equipped with sufficient number of CROs, Digital storage oscilloscopes etc.,


Specifications:

  • PI, PID Controller Kits
  • Measuring Instruments
  • DC Servomotor position control
  • Stepper motors
  • Lag and Lead Networks
  • AC Servomotor Kit
  • Servo Modular Kit
Measurement & Instrumentation Laboratory

Specifications:

  • Signal Sampling equipments
  • Process flow and Pressure controller
  • Temperature controller
  • Transducers
  • AC and DC Bridges
Power System Simulation Laboratory

Specifications:

  • MATLAB
  • PSIM

Power Electronics and Drives Laboratory

  1. Gate Pulse Generation using R, RC and UJT
  2. Characteristics of SCR and Triac
  3. Characteristics of MOSFET and IGBT
  4. AC to DC half controlled converter
